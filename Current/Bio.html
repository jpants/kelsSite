NOTHING IS WASTED
A little about me... My name is Kelsie Elfrink, and my hope in life is that I can be real and authentic in whatever I do. I get to be married to the best man I know, Joel, and he is the greatest gift I've ever been given. My relationship with Jesus is of the utmost importance to me, and my family is comprised of my favorite people on earth. I love to write songs, blogs, and random notes about what goes on in my head. I don't feel like I have a lot figured out, but I'm trying to learn and grow each day. Lately I've been working on being less lazy in my free time, being creative in all that I do, and learning the cello. I love to water ski, snowboard, play music with my man, and enjoy a good game night. Sitting next to a fire is the best thing in life and it makes anything I love that much better. I hope that I love people well and live a life that is connected to God.
