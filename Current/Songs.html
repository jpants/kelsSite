SONGS

Take Back

This song I wrote for people who love justice. It talks about how we can often be unfaithful in life, but there is a way to find healing from that.

I Want Life

I love the story where people are walking away from Jesus, and he turns to the disciples and asks if they are going to leave too. Peter's response is so beautiful. "Lord, to whom shall we go? You have the words of eternal life." This song tries to capture that story.

Run Away

It's easy for me to be overwhelmed by the future and I often fear missing out or making the wrong decision. This song goes to the heart of that fear and reveals the way to fight it.

Your People

A song for those who wonder and wander and need to know that they are still loved.
